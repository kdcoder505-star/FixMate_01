How it works

1
Submit Complaint

Student logs in and submits a complaint with category, priority, and optional photos.

2
Admin Review & Assign

Admin reviews the issue on the dashboard and assigns it to the relevant department (e.g., IT, Maintenance).

3
Resolution

The issue is fixed, status is updated to 'Resolved', and the student is notified.
